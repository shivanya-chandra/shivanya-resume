Selected Projects

Collegify — College Planning Platform

Collegify was designed to help students navigate the college application process using data-driven recommendations. The platform considers academic performance, financial constraints, and personal interests to suggest schools that align with a student’s goals.

I worked across the full stack, integrating a TensorFlow-based recommendation engine while ensuring that results were interpretable and user-friendly. This project strengthened my ability to balance model accuracy with usability and explain technical decisions clearly.

TrainHer — Self-Defense Training App

Built during a hackathon, TrainHer uses camera-based motion tracking to provide real-time feedback on self-defense techniques. The goal was to make training more accessible and engaging.

I focused on motion tracking logic, Firebase authentication, and real-time feedback delivery. The project placed second among 30+ teams and emphasized rapid prototyping under time constraints.

BoilerFind — Mentor Matching Platform

BoilerFind helps Purdue students connect with mentors based on shared interests and academic goals. I contributed to the matching algorithm and implemented secure user verification using Purdue emails.
